
Data will be gathered through probe detection, and at the same time, cars seen will be recorded via a simple program. 
These tests will help determine accuracy of the cars detected through probe requests. 

Information that should be documented when testing the device near roads include:

- Name of teammember implementing the test
- Location of where the test is being completed
- Type of road
- Date and time of test 
- Length of test 
- Actual cars seen
- Cars detected
- Any other comments that are relevant to analyze information
